Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Phoenix Studio ("we", "our", or "us") built the Words on Beat app (the "App") as a freemium application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

The App uses anonymous authentication through Firebase to provide its services. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name or email address.

Audio Recording

The App uses your device's microphone to record audio for the purpose of speech-to-text transcription and pronunciation scoring. Audio recordings are temporarily uploaded to our servers for processing and are automatically deleted after transcription is complete. We do not store, share, or use your audio recordings for any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

- Google Play Services
- Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Functions, Cloud Storage)
- OpenAI Whisper API (audio transcription)
- Google AdMob (advertising)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of information they collect.

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases processed through Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play and is not collected or stored by us.

Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
